Institutional Workshops

Programs for universities, colleges, and institutions: lecture-demonstrations, hands-on residencies, and culturally grounded talks in Puerto Rican Bomba.

Ideal for music, dance, Latinx/Caribbean studies, cultural competency trainings, chapels, and cultural initiatives. Content adapts to course outcomes and audience level.

Sample Outcomes

  • Lecture-demo: history, rhythm family (sicá, yubá, cuembé, holandé), call-and-response
  • Hands-on: basic step/paseo, ensemble coordination, cultural context
  • Residency options: multi-day modules, culminating share or performance
  • Audience: students, faculty, and community partners
  • Logistics: on-site space, sound, and travel coordination

FAQ

Do I need my own drum?

No. If you have one, bring it. If not, we’ll adapt with practice surfaces and technique.

Are lessons in Spanish or English?

Either—your choice. The tradition is Puerto Rican; we honor both languages.

Where are lessons held?

In-person (DFW area) or we can arrange a suitable space. No virtual lessons listed by default.
